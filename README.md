# Czurtech Scanner Guide

Created by Autumn Painter

1. Turn on the computer and scanner
2. On the desktop, click the Czurtech Scanner Program on desktop
<img src="img/image1.jpg" width="80%"> 
3. Allow it to make changes
<img src="img/image2.jpg" width="80%"> 
4. Choose scanner
<img src="img/image3.jpg" width="80%"> 
5. Go to settings in the top right corner. This is where you can change your DPI, frequency, Resolution, etc. 
<img src="img/image4.jpg" width="80%"> 
<img src="img/image5.jpg" width="80%"> 
6. Click scan in the top left corner
<img src="img/image6.jpg" width="80%"> 
7. Adjust the location of the book, make your color/processing selections
<img src="img/image7.jpg" width="80%"> 
8. Press the foot pedal to scan
9. Once you have finished scanning close out of the window by clicking the 'X' in the top. This will bring you back to the original window. You should see each page that is scanned in the right sidebar. Select all the pages in the sidebar, then press 'Export' on the top menu. You can choose between PDF, TIFF, OCR, and Searchable PDF. 
<img src="img/image8.jpg" width="80%"> 
10. Save to your flashdrive. 
<img src="img/image9.jpg" width="80%">
